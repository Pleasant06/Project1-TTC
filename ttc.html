WELCOME TO CHURCH

Hello and welcome to The Transforming Church website. It’s
always a real joy for us to meet and have new members in our
church family. Here, you will find useful information about our
beliefs. Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship
with Jesus Christ. Please join our church and start a new life.
Whatever that next step is, it’s time to take a bold step of faith
from your comfort zone and discover what God has for you. We are
on believe that you can’t go with God and stay where you are. We are
One Church Making Global Impact.

Rev'd Sam Oye

Lead Pastor
